# The Rhetorician

> I am thou, thou art I...
>
> Unworthy is the rule whose subjects seek the solace of false gods

![cece](/assets/images/cece.jpg)

The magic of The Rhetorician ensures you always have certain spells ready as described in the Rhetorician Spells table. Additionally you may cast any of these spells without using a spell slot, and without the need of material components, using your **Steps of the Fey** subclass feature
