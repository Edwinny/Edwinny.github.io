## Map of The Creator

Your Star Map reveals an additional constellation known as the Creator, its pattern ever-shifting among the heavens.

When you use your **Starry Form** feature, you can invoke the power of the Creator. If you do, you manifest the traits of two constellations simultaneously instead of one. Choose any two from Archer, Chalice, or Dragon; you gain the benefits of both for the duration of that use of Starry Form.

Once this property has been used, it can’t be used again until you finish a long rest.
