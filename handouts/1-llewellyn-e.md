## Emissary’s Vow

When you use your **Vow of Enmity** Channel Divinity feature, you can invoke the power of the Emissary. If you do, your attack is a guaranteed critical hit.

In addition, the attack immediately triggers Divine Smite without expending a spell slot or requiring any action. This Divine Smite deals damage as though you had expended a spell slot of a level equal to your proficiency bonus.

Once this property has been used, it can’t be used again until you finish a long rest.
